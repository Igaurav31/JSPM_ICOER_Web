About us

Prof. Dr. T. J. Sawant, is a person with lot of ambitions, with capacity to achieve them. In 1998, he initiated the formation of public charitable trust “Jayawant Shikshan Prasarak Mandal”, with a vision to establish quality educational institutes in Pune.

The First unit of JSPM started in the year 2001 under the “Rajarshi Shahu College of Engineering” at Tathawade, a developing suburb in PCMC area near software park.

At the Inception, the college got permission for 150 students only. It was the dream of Prof. Dr. T. J. Sawant to see that the college gets best results from the first batch, in University of Pune. Many new and innovative methods were implemented to achieve the academic excellence.The dream came true and in the year 2013 the college achieved the award from University of Pune as “Best Professional College”. The college received National Board of Accreditation certificate for creating best academic and infrastructural facilities.

Through JSPM, Prof. Dr. T. J. Sawant established educational campus at Tathawade, Hadapsar, Wagholi, Bavdhan, Narhe at various suburbs of Pune City. Since 2014, campus is functioning at Barshi (A Town in Solapur District). All these campuses are having quality educational Institutes, schools and Colleges with excellent Infrastructure and Academic ambience.

Currently JSPM is widely spread organization with 7 Campuses, 78 Institutes, 10000 Staff, 400 Buses, 50000 Students. Prof. Dr. T.J.Sawant is aiming to start well equipped state-of-art Hospital and Medical college on one of the campuses. Establishing a Private University is also an ambitious project of our Founder Secretary Prof. Dr. T.J.Sawant.
